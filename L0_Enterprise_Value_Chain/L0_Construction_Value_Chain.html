1. Purpose & Scope

This is the Level 0 enterprise view of the South African construction and property development industry. It decomposes the full project lifecycle into 7 value-chain phases and identifies the 8 professional domains that operate across these phases. Every downstream document (L1→L2→L3→L4) traces back to this map.

BPMN Decomposition: L0 (this document) → L1 (8 Professional Domains) → L2 (Process Areas per domain) → L3 (Sub-Processes per PA) → L4 (Tasks per sub-process). Each level links to its parent and children.

2. Value Chain Phases

A construction project passes through 7 sequential phases. Each professional domain (L1) contributes to one or more phases.

Phase 1

Feasibility & Inception

Site analysis, feasibility studies, funding model, project charter

Phase 2

Design & Documentation

Concept → detailed design, BoQ production, regulatory submissions

Phase 3

Procurement & Tender

Tender docs, contractor evaluation, contract award, mobilisation

Phase 4

Construction & Delivery

Site execution, valuations, variations, quality, H&S, programme

Phase 5

Handover & Commissioning

Practical completion, snag lists, testing, O&M handover

Phase 6

Close-out & Final Account

Final measurement, final account, retention release, archive

Phase 7

Asset & Warranty

Defects liability, warranty management, asset registration
