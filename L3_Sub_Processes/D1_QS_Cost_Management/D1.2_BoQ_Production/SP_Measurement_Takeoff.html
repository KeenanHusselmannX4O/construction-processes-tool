1. Purpose

Systematically measure all building elements from finalised construction drawings following ASAQS standard method of measurement. This is the most labour-intensive QS activity and produces the quantified basis for the Bills of Quantities.

Trigger
Finalised construction drawings issued for BoQ
Standard
ASAQS Standard System of Measuring Building Work (7th Ed)
Output
Complete dimension sheets / quantity schedules
