Elemental Cost Plan

L1: QS & Cost Management → L2: Cost Planning & Estimation → L3: Elemental Cost Plan
Process ID: D1.1-SP-002 Value Chain: Phase 2 (Design & Documentation) Version 2.0 April 2026
↑ L2 D1.1 Cost Planning ← Feasibility Estimate ● SP: Elemental Cost Plan → Cost Plan Revision ↓ L4 Tasks

1. Purpose

Develop a detailed elemental cost breakdown aligned to ASAQS standard elements as design progresses. The elemental cost plan is the primary cost control document through design, enabling design-to-budget decisions before procurement.

Trigger
Concept design approved; detailed design commences
Accuracy Range
±10–15% (ASAQS Class 3 estimate)
Output
Elemental cost plan with assumptions register
