Monthly Cost Report for Funder

1. Purpose

Compile a comprehensive monthly cost report for funders, development finance institutions (DFIs), and investors detailing: project expenditure to date, progress against programme, projected final cost (PFC), drawdown utilisation, and flagging any cost or time risk items. Funder reports are a contractual obligation when external finance is in place and must align with the funder's prescribed reporting template.

Trigger

Monthly reporting deadline per funder's loan agreement (typically 5–10 business days after month-end)

Accuracy

Report must reconcile to within R0 variance of the latest payment certificate and project financial model

Output

Monthly Cost Report (funder template); Expenditure summary; PFC; Risk register update; Supporting schedules
