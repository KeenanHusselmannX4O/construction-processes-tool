1. Purpose

Prepare and submit drawdown requests to the funder or development finance institution (DFI) to release funds from the approved facility for paying contractors, consultants, and other project costs. The QS certifies the expenditure, confirms work-in-place, and packages the supporting documentation required by the funder's drawdown conditions. Drawdowns must comply with the loan agreement's conditions precedent and utilisation schedule.

Trigger

Payment certificate issued (D1.5) or milestone payment due per loan agreement schedule

Standard

Funder loan agreement; Development finance conditions precedent; SA FICA / banking regulations

Output

Signed drawdown request; QS certification; Supporting documentation pack; Drawdown register update
