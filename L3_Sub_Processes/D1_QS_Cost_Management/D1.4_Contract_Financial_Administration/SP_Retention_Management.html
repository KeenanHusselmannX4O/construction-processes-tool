Retention Management

1. Purpose

Manage retention deductions throughout construction and ensure timely release at practical completion and final completion. Retention serves as security for the employer against the contractor's obligation to complete defects. The QS must track retention held, ensure it does not exceed the contractual limit, process release upon milestones, and manage retention guarantee substitutions where applicable.

Trigger

Each monthly payment certificate (deduction); practical completion / final completion (release)

Standard

JBCC clause 32 (Retention); NEC clause 51.3; typical 5% deduction capped at 5% of contract sum

Output

Retention schedule per certificate; release certificates at practical and final completion
