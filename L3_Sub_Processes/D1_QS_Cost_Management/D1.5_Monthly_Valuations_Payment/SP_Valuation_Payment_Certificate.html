Valuation Calculation & Payment Certificate

L1: QS & Cost Management → L2: Monthly Valuations & Payment → L3: Valuation & Payment Certificate
Process ID: D1.5-SP-002Value Chain: Phase 4–5Version 2.0April 2026
↑ L2 D1.5 Valuations ← Site Measurement ● SP: Valuation Certificate ↓ L4 Tasks

1. Purpose

Calculate the interim payment certificate value from site measurement records by compiling all components: measured work, variations, CPA, materials on site, provisional sum adjustments, and deductions (retention, penalties, previous payments). The payment certificate is a contractual document that obligates the employer to pay the contractor within the stipulated period.

Trigger

Site measurement records compiled (D1.5-SP-001 output); monthly valuation date reached

Standard

JBCC clause 31 (Payment) / NEC clause 50; payment within 30 days of certificate issue

Output

Signed Interim Payment Certificate with supporting valuation schedule
