D1.5 — Sub-Process

Site Measurement & Progress Assessment

L1: QS & Cost Management → L2: Monthly Valuations & Payment → L3: Site Measurement & Progress
Process ID: D1.5-SP-001Value Chain: Phase 4–5Version 2.0April 2026
↑ L2 D1.5 Valuations ● SP: Site Measurement → Valuation Certificate ↓ L4 Tasks

1. Purpose

Conduct monthly site visits to measure work completed and assess progress against the BoQ. This physical verification forms the basis of the interim valuation and payment certificate. The QS must reconcile contractor claims against actual site progress via joint measurement where required.

Trigger

Monthly valuation date (typically 25th of each month or per contract agreement)

Accuracy

Within ±3% of actual quantities; reconciled to contractor's claim schedule

Output

Site measurement records; progress percentages per BoQ section; photographs as evidence
