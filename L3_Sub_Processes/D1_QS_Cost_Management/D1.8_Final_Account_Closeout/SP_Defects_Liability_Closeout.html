D1.8 — Sub-Process

Defects Liability Financial Close-out

L1: QS & Cost Management → L2: Final Account & Close-out → L3: Defects Liability Close-out
Process ID: D1.8-SP-002Value Chain: Phase 7Version 2.0April 2026
↑ L2 D1.8 Final Account ← Final Measurement ● SP: Defects Liability ↓ L4 Tasks

1. Purpose

Manage the financial aspects of the defects liability period (DLP) from practical completion to final completion: administer retention release schedules, cost defect rectification works if the contractor fails to remedy, reconcile any outstanding financial matters, and issue the final completion certificate to achieve definitive financial close-out of the contract.

Trigger

Practical Completion certified; defects liability period commences (typically 3–12 months per contract)

Standard

JBCC clause 26 (Defects Liability); clause 34.7 (Second Moiety); NEC clause 45

Output

DLP cost register; Second moiety release; Final completion certificate; Contractual financial close-out
