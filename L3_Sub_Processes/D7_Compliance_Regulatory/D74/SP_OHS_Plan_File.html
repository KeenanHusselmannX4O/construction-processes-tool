1. Purpose

Baseline risk assessment, fall protection plan, safety file content per CR 2014. This sub-process defines the structured approach for delivering this component of OHS Act Compliance within the Compliance & Regulatory domain, ensuring compliance with applicable South African standards, professional body requirements, and project quality expectations.
