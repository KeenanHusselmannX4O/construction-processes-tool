1. Process Area Purpose

Cost Planning & Estimation establishes the project's financial framework from inception through construction. It translates architectural designs into monetary terms, providing stakeholders with progressive cost certainty. This PA produces the cost estimates and cost plans that drive budgets, funding applications, and financial viability assessments throughout the project lifecycle.

Standards: ASAQS Standard System of Measuring Building Work (7th Ed.) · SACQSP Practice Standards · NRM1 (reference) · JBCC Series 2000

3. Inputs & Outputs

Inputs

  • Architectural brief, sketch plans, working drawings
  • Geotechnical report (from D4 Engineering)
  • Previous project cost data & benchmarks
  • BER Building Cost Index / Rode's Report
  • Funder requirements & subsidy thresholds (NHBRC/DHS)
  • Site conditions, topography, bulk services proximity

Outputs

  • Feasibility cost estimate (± 20-30% accuracy)
  • Elemental cost plan (± 10-15% accuracy)
  • Revised cost plan (± 5-10% accuracy)
  • Cost plan comparison reports
  • Escalation schedules
  • Input to BoQ production (→ D1.2) & budget tracking (→ D1.7)
