1. Process Area Purpose

Final Account & Close-out covers the preparation and agreement of the final contract sum after all work is complete. It involves final measurement of all work executed, reconciliation of every variation, adjustment, and provisional sum, and compilation of the comprehensive final account. The defects liability period administration ensures all defects are rectified before final completion certificate issue and retention release.

Standards: JBCC Series 2000 Clauses 32-35 · NEC4 Clause 50 · ASAQS Practice Standards · SACQSP Code of Conduct
