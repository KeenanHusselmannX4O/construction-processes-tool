1. Domain Overview

Construction Management covers all physical execution activities on site — from mobilisation through practical completion. This domain is responsible for translating design documents into the built product safely, efficiently, and to the specified quality. The construction manager/contractor manages resources (labour, plant, materials), subcontractors, and the construction programme.

Regulatory Bodies: SACPCMP (Pr.CM) · CIDB (Contractor grading) · DoEL (OHS Act & Construction Regulations 2014) · Standards: ISO 9001 (Quality), ISO 45001 (H&S), Construction Regulations 2014
