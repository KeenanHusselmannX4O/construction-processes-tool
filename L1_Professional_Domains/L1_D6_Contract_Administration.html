1. Domain Overview

Contract Administration covers the formal management of contractual relationships between employer, contractor, and consultants. The Principal Agent (PA) administers the building contract, issues instructions, manages claims and disputes, certifies completion, and ensures all parties fulfil their contractual obligations. This domain is the legal backbone of every construction project.

Contract Forms: JBCC Series 2000 (Edition 6.2) · NEC4 · FIDIC · GCC 2015 · Key Role: Principal Agent (JBCC) / Project Manager (NEC) / Engineer (FIDIC)
