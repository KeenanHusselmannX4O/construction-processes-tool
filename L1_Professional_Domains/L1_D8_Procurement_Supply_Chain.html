1. Domain Overview

Procurement & Supply Chain governs how materials, equipment, specialist subcontractors, and services are sourced, evaluated, and delivered to the construction project. It spans strategic procurement planning during design through to on-site material logistics during construction. In the South African affordable housing context, procurement must also satisfy B-BBEE requirements, CIDB contractor grading, and preferential procurement targets.

Regulatory Framework: CIDB Standard for Uniformity · PPPFA 5/2000 · B-BBEE Codes of Good Practice · SABS Standards · Supply Chain Models: Design-Bid-Build · Design & Build · Management Contracting · Nominated Subcontractors
