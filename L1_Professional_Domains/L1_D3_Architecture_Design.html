1. Domain Overview

Architecture & Design covers the creative and technical design of the built environment — from concept development through construction documentation. Architects translate client briefs into buildable design solutions that satisfy functional, aesthetic, regulatory, and contextual requirements.

Regulatory Body: SACAP (South African Council for the Architectural Profession) · Registration: Pr.Arch / Pr.S.Arch / Pr.D(Arch) · Standards: SANS 10400 (National Building Regulations), SANS 204 (Energy), Universal Design
