1. Domain Overview

The Compliance & Regulatory domain ensures that every construction project complies with South Africa's extensive legislative framework — from municipal building plan approvals and NHBRC enrolment through to OHS Act site safety, environmental impact assessments, and B-BBEE scorecard compliance. This domain runs as a continuous governance thread across all value chain phases.

Regulatory Bodies: Municipality Building Control · NHBRC · DoEL (OHS) · DFFE (Environmental) · CIDB · Department of Labour · Key Acts: National Building Regulations & Standards Act 103/1977 · OHS Act 85/1993 · Construction Regulations 2014 · NEMA 107/1998 · B-BBEE Act 53/2003
