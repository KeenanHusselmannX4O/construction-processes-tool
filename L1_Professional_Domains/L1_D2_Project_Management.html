1. Domain Overview

Project Management encompasses the planning, coordination, monitoring, and control of all project activities from inception to close-out. The PM is the central orchestrator ensuring that the project is delivered on time, within budget, to the required quality, and in compliance with all contractual and statutory requirements.

Regulatory Body: SACPCMP (South African Council for the Project and Construction Management Professions) · Registration: Pr.PM / Pr.CM · Standard: PMBOK 7th Edition, ISO 21500
