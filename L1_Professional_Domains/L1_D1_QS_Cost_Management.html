1. Domain Overview

The Quantity Surveying & Cost Management domain encompasses all financial management activities on a construction project, from initial feasibility through to final account close-out. The QS acts as the financial steward, ensuring that construction costs are planned, controlled, certified, and reported accurately throughout the project lifecycle.

Regulatory Bodies & Standards

SACQSP — South African Council for the Quantity Surveying Profession · ASAQS — Association of South African Quantity Surveyors · RICS — Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (international) · Standard Method of Measurement — ASAQS 7th Edition · Contract Forms — JBCC Series 2000 (6.2), NEC4, FIDIC
